How to use Trippy

To Create a New Itinerary

  • The default location is your current location.
  • To change your location, click on 'change your location' and a dialog box will appear. Enter the location where you would like to seach in the dialog box and click enter. You will now be searching for venues in the new location.
  • Click the create a new link.
  • Click 'Add an Item'
  • Enter something to search for in the search box in top left. You can search for something general, such as 'restaurant', 'nightlife', or 'art,' or something more specific, such as 'bagels', 'dive bars', or 'science museum.'
  • Your search results will appear in the pane on the left. Click on the + next to any venue to add it your itinerary.
  • Click the '<' and '>' buttons to move through the results.
  • You can add multiple venues to your itinerary.
  • When you add a venue to your itinerary it's map marker changes to a pin.
  • Once added, a venue in the search results will appear grey and have the words 'Added to Itinerary' and 'Remove from Itinerary' on it.
  • You can also remove a venue from your itinerary. Click on the 'Remove from Itinerary' link and the venue will no longer appear in your itinerary.
  • You can view your itinerary at any time by simply clicking on the 'View itinerary' button that appears at the bottom of the search results.
  • When you have finished seaching and adding venues to you list, you can view and reorde your itinerary. To reorder an itinerary, click the 'View itinerary' button. Once you are in the itinerary view mode you can reorder your itinerary by simply dragging a venue to the location on the list where you would like it to be. You new itinerary and map are then updated.
  • When you ae satisfied with an itinerary, you may save it by clicking the 'SAVE' button on the top right of the application. You then give your application a name and hit enter. Your itinerary is then saved. You can share a saved itinerary by sending it to whomever you wish to share it with.
  • If you ever make a mistake, you can hit 'cancel' to exit what you were doing.

To Load a Saved Itinerary

  • If you want to continue working on a saved itinerary, simply click the 'load a saved itinerary.'
  • You will be see a list of saved itineraries. Click on the name of the itinerary you want to open and your itinerary will open in itinerary view.
  • The functionality is now the same as if you has created a new itinerary.
  • Have fun!
